[center][color=#00ff00][size=6]Standalone Hidden Helmets[/size][/color][/center]
[color=#00ff00][size=4] [u]Description:[/u][/size][/color]

 Adds [color=#ffd966]standalone invisible helmets[/color] for each in-game armor set and [color=#ffd966]combinable S.P.E.C.I.A.L. enhancing armor[/color] pieces meant to replace hats so you can get the [color=#ffd966]stat bonuses yet keep[/color] looking at your stylish [color=#ffd966]character's face[/color]. Can be used too [color=#ffd966]in combination with style gear[/color].

 The helmets added are literally an exact copy of their original ones with the only difference they don't change the character's appearance and they [b]don't take up armor slots[/b]. This means they [b]can be modified the same exact[/b] way and have the same available modifications and requirements. S.P.E.C.I.A.L. enhancing armor, like hats, can't be upgraded.

 As for now the only way to get this items is via the console. I can't provide their IDs since they depend on the ESP load order.
Run [color=#00ff00]help hidden 4[/color], locate your desired helmet id and then execute [color=#00ff00]player.additem [/color][color=#ffd966][i]idGoesHere[/i] [/color][color=#00ff00]1[/color].
